HOW TO MAKE BROWNIES

ingridients

  • Cocoa Powder
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Milk
  • Butter

instructions
  1. Preaheat Oven to 350 F
  2. In a large bowl Add the Ingridients
  3. Melt butter in pan
  4. Add both sugars in pan till it melts
  5. mix dry ingridients with butter and sugar/butter
  6. add milk in the bowl
  7. add it into the tray and bake for 40 minutes
